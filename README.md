# Pixel Phones - Android 15 / 16 Setup Notes

Personal notes to setup brand new Pixel phones running Android 15 / 16

### Boot to device recovery

Hold Power button and Volume down to boot to Fastboot (During initial boot or restart)

In Fastboot, select Recovery mode. On "No command" screen, hold Power button and press Volume up once

### Physical button screenshot

Tap Power Button + Volume Down

###  Long Press Home Screen > Wallpaper and style

---

* Colors > Choose colour
* Color contrast > High
* Layout > Small

###  Long Press Home Screen > Home Settings

---

* At a Glance - Off
* Swipe to access Google app - Off
* Overview suggestions - Off
* Suggestions
  * Suggestions in all app list - Off
  * Suggestions on Home screen - Off
* Search settings - Everything Off

### System Settings

---

* Network and Internet > Internet > Scroll to bottom
    * Network Preferences
        * Notify for public networks - Off
        * Allow WEP - Off
    * Carrier Name > Allow 2G - Off

---

* Connected devices > Connection preferences
    * Bluetooth > Off
    * Bluetooth > Automatically turn on tomorrow > Off
    * NFC > Off

---

* Notifications
    * App notifications > Turn on only necessary apps
    * Notification History - On

---

* Sound and vibration
    * Volume sliders - Max all
    * Vibrations and haptics - Off
    * Tap & click sounds - Off

---

* Display & touch
    * Screen timeout > Set to 10 minutes
    * Lock Screen
        * Tap to check phone - Off
        * Lift to check phone - Off
    * Smooth display (bottom of page) - On

---

* Battery
    * Battery Health > Charging optimization > Limit to 80%
    * Battery percentage - On

---

* System
  * Life Translate - Off
  * Software updates
      * System update > Latest
      * Google Play system update > Latest
  * Gestures
      * Press and hold power button > Access power menu

---

* Security and Privacy
  * Privacy controls > Camera Access, Microphone Access - Off
  * More security and privacy
    * Personalize using app data - Off
    * Android System Intelligence > Customize the experience using your Google Account data - Off

---

* Google Play system update (Android 15 / 16)
  * Security and Privacy > System & updates > Google Play system update
